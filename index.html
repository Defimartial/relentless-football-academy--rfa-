Our Journey

Founded in 2018, Relentless Football Academy emerged from the strong foundation of Immanuel Football Academy in Jos. We rebranded to RFA to reflect our commitment to relentless pursuit of excellence in football development.

Our academy is dedicated to nurturing young talent, building character, and developing world-class footballers who compete at the highest levels. We combine technical football training with personal development and academic support.

At RFA, we believe that every player has the potential to become a champion. Our coaching staff, training facilities, and holistic approach ensure that our players not only excel on the field but also grow as responsible individuals.
